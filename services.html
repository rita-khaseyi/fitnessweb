OUR SERVICES

Mixed martial Arts

Our sports center offers The core of our training is split into three components. Tae kwon do, kempo, and Lee Jun Fan Gung Fu. From no belt to green belt we study tae kwon do. This is a Korean martial art, which also has numerous styles. We study three: mu duk kwon (china fist), chun do kwon (blue cottage), and yang do wan (young dragon).

GYM

Rita's sports center is Not just a normal gym, but a gym that offers personal training from numerous personal trainers that are available at all hours. it also offer additional options such as, group classes, personalized prepared meals from a nutrition specialist, and an on-site protein shake cafe. It allows clients to be active and socialize in the same location where they are improving their nutrition.
